# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We pledge to make participation in this project and community a harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment:

- Using welcoming and inclusive language
- Being respectful of differing viewpoints and experiences
- Giving and accepting constructive feedback
- Focusing on user outcomes and evidence-driven discussion

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- Harassment, threats, insulting or derogatory comments
- Publishing private information without explicit permission
- Trolling, deliberate disruption, or bad-faith behavior

## Enforcement Responsibilities

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing our standards of acceptable behavior.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies in all project spaces, including issues, pull requests, discussions, and community interactions related to this repository.

## Reporting

Report incidents privately to project maintainers through GitHub.

All reports will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly.

This document is adapted from the Contributor Covenant, version 2.1.
